Smart Workouts

Workout planning designed around your goals and experience level

Personalized Exercise Selection

Revon Fitness helps you build workout routines based on your fitness goals, current experience level, and the equipment you have available. Choose from pre-made workout templates or create your own custom routines.

The app includes a library of exercises with written descriptions to help you understand proper form and technique.

Core Features

  • Workout templates for common goals like strength building, general fitness, and weight loss
  • Filter exercises by equipment type (gym, home, bodyweight)
  • Track sets, reps, and weight for each exercise
  • Exercise library with form descriptions
  • Built-in rest timer between sets
  • View your workout history and track consistency

Training Options

Access workout templates for different training styles including strength training, bodyweight exercises, and cardio routines. All workouts can be customized to fit your schedule and preferences.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your fitness progress over time

Track Your Progress

Log your workouts and track key metrics over time. Revon Fitness helps you see how you're improving by keeping a record of your training sessions and body measurements.

View simple charts that show your progress trends, making it easier to stay motivated and identify when you need to adjust your training.

What You Can Track

  • Body weight and basic measurements
  • Personal records for exercises you perform regularly
  • Total workout volume and training frequency
  • Cardio activities (distance, time, pace)
  • Progress photos to compare over time
  • Workout streak to track consistency
  • Set custom fitness goals

Performance History

Review your workout history to see how you've progressed. Compare your current performance to previous weeks and months to understand what's working in your training routine.

Nutrition Guide

Basic nutrition tracking to support your fitness goals

Meal Planning & Tracking

Nutrition plays an important role in fitness results. Revon Fitness includes basic tools to help you track what you eat and ensure you're meeting your nutritional needs.

Set calorie and macro targets based on your goals, then log your meals to see how you're doing. The app provides general guidance on nutrition for fitness.

Nutrition Tools

  • Calculate daily calorie needs based on your activity level
  • Set macronutrient targets (protein, carbs, fats)
  • Log meals and track daily food intake
  • Access a database of common foods
  • Save frequently eaten meals for quick logging
  • Track daily water intake

Food Logging

Keep a simple food diary to monitor your eating habits. Search the food database, save your favorite meals, and view your daily nutrition totals to help stay on track with your goals.

Nutritional Guidance

Access basic nutrition information and guidelines for supporting your fitness goals. Learn about proper portion sizes, macronutrients, and meal timing concepts.
